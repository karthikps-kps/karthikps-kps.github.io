About me

Hello there! I'm a Mechanical Engineer with a Bachelor's degree from APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University. However, my professional journey has led me to develop a passion for data analytics and business strategy. My journey as a data enthusiast began while working as a Composite Engineer at Creedx Composites Pvt Ltd, where I not only monitored product development but also delved into the world of business analytics using tools like Google Analytics. Eventually, I advanced to the position of Business Development Manager. This experience was transformative, sparking my interest in data analytics.

Previously, as a Customer Relationship Officer at JSW Paints Pvt Ltd, I spearheaded the development and implementation of a B2B sales funnel, resulting in a significant boost in sales. This success propelled me into assisting service sector startups in business development, where I navigated the challenges of launching services into the market by employing strategic analysis of the existing market.

My professional toolkit encompasses market research, cross-functional team leadership, and proficiency in Python, Power BI, Tableau, and data analytics with MS Excel. I've led non-technical projects as a Project Coordinator at Zilicon Technologies Pvt Ltd, ensuring customer satisfaction within tight timelines. Recently, I pursued a Certified Specialist in Data Science and Analytics from the ICT Academy of Kerala and, armed with a Six Sigma White Belt Certification, I have also completed an internship with TCSion, developing a machine learning model for Demand Forecasting.

And now, I've embarked on a Postgraduate Diploma course in Data Analytics for Business Decision Making at Durham College in Canada seeking to formalize my understanding of the subject and gain insights into the international business world. My sights are set on becoming a Business Analyst or Data Analyst, utilizing my skills to make impactful contributions.

I'm particularly drawn to the challenging and dynamic retail business, where precise data analysis can drive optimal decision-making. My ultimate goal is to leverage my mechanical engineering background and data analytics expertise to make a mark in this ever-evolving landscape.
